Frequently Asked Questions

What are digital collectibles?

+

Digital collectibles are unique digital assets that represent ownership of exclusive content from your favorite artists. They're secured on the blockchain, making them authentic, verifiable, and truly yours.

How do I purchase collectibles?

+

Once launched, you'll be able to purchase collectibles directly through Starlight+ using various payment methods. Early registrants will get first access to limited editions.

Can I trade or sell my collectibles?

+

Yes! You can trade collectibles with other fans in our secure marketplace or hold onto them as they may increase in value over time.

What makes these collectibles special?

+

Our collectibles feature exclusive, never-before-seen content directly from the artists, limited edition releases, and come with special perks and rewards for owners.
