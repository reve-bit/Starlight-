Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a Starlight+ account?

+

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of our homepage, enter your email address, create a password, and follow the verification steps. You'll be ready to explore Starlight+ in minutes!

Is there a free trial available?

+

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial for new users. You'll have access to all premium features during this period. No credit card is required to start your trial.

How can I participate in voting events?

+

To participate in voting events, you need an active Starlight+ account. Navigate to the "Community" section, find ongoing voting events, and follow the instructions. Some events may require earned or purchased voting tokens.

Can I download content to watch offline?

+

Yes, premium subscribers can download select content for offline viewing. Look for the download icon on compatible videos. Note that some exclusive live content may not be available for download due to licensing restrictions.

How do I cancel my subscription?

+

You can cancel your subscription at any time by going to your Account Settings, selecting "Subscription", and clicking "Cancel Subscription". Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

What devices are supported?

+

Starlight+ is available on iOS, Android, Windows, and macOS. You can also access our web platform through all modern browsers. Our mobile apps are available on the App Store and Google Play Store.
